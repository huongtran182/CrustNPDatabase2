Frequently Asked Questions

What is the definition of neuropeptide?

While there are many controversial definitions about neuropeptides, we consider one as a neuropeptide to add into this database if they have the following characteristics: 1. Must be synthesized and released by a neuron; 2. Endogenous, small protein-like molecule composed of short chains of amino acids that function as signaling molecules in the nervous system; 3. Derived from a neuropeptide prohormones/precursors.
